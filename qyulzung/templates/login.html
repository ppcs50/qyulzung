Welcome to Qyulzung! Have you ever heard about this word before? I guess not, since it's not a familiar word as "Kimchi". It is literally means 'Decision'. Just pronunciate it whatever you want, and when you met some Korean friends, ask how properly pronunciate. They will gladly teach you and definitely smile while you trying to pronunciate. :)
This is a web application for a decision-making worksheet, based on the famous rational decision-making model. It's very simple and there is no Magic. It just provides you a step by step form and calculate what you input, and save it to our account, so that whenever you want you can review your previous decision history.
Also, if you want you can post your Decision log to the board. It is opened to public. Share your decision and take advantage of inspiring by someone else's one, that is the main purpose of this application.
