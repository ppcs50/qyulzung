About
"It's difficult to be rational, when you try to consider various factors simultaneously."
Welcome to Qyulzung! Have you ever heard about this word before? I guess not, since it's not a familiar word as "Kimchi". Qyulzung is literally means 'Decision' in Korean. Try to pronunciate it and when you met Korean friends, ask them how properly pronunciate it. They will gladly teach you and definitely smile at you while you trying to pronunciate it. :)
This is a web application for a decision-making worksheet, based on the famous rational decision-making model. It's very simple and there is no Magic. It just provides you a step by step model and calculate what you input, and save it to our account, so that whenever you want you can review your previous decision history.
Also, if you want you can post your Decision log to the board. It is opened to public. Share your decision and take advantage of inspiring by someone else's one, that is the main purpose of this application.
